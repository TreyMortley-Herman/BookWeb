About The Book Web

The Book Web is an application designed to help you discover, track, and organize your reading journey. You can browse books by genre, add them to your reading list, and keep track of books you've finished.

Whether you're a casual reader or a book enthusiast, The Book Web provides a user-friendly interface to enhance your reading experience. Explore our extensive library, filter books by genre, and create a personalized reading list to keep track of your progress. With The Book Web, you'll never run out of great reads! Discover new authors, genres, and titles that match your interests.
